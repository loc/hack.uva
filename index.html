FAQ

Have any questions? Email us at hackersuva-contact@virginia.edu!

  • What is Hack.UVA?

    A programming event that will be held on Saturday, March 24, 2018 to Sunday, March 25th, 2018 where you, along with other developers, designers, and entrepreneurs work together to create a web or mobile application/platform from scratch. Prizes will be awarded to the top hacks. There is no entrance fee and all meals are free .

  • Who can attend?

    Hack.UVA welcomes all current students. If you are an organization interested in sponsoring the event, please contact hackersuva-contact@virginia.edu.

  • When can I register?

    Registration is now open, you can apply here!

  • How big can my team be?

    Between 3 and 7 feet tall. But seriously, you may work in a team with up to four people.

  • Can I come even if I'm inexperienced?

    Hack.UVA is a great opportunity for new hackers. There will be tech talks and personal support from mentors at the event.

  • Will there be travel reimbursement?

    We will be providing busses to most large Universites around UVA this year. We will also do travel reimbursements on a case by case basis.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your laptop, phone, and chargers!

  • How does judging work?

    The judges will evaluate your hack based off creativity, polish, and technical vigor. Projects will also be judged on how well they embody the theme they are competing in. Succeed in all three categories, and you're bound to win!

  • Is there code of conduct?

    Yes, we abide by the Major League Hacking Code of Conduct .
