March 24 - March 25, 2018

ABOUT

Hack.UVA is a programming event hosted by Hackers@UVA that will be held on March 24 - March 25, 2018 where you, along with other developers, designers, and entrepreneurs work together to create a web or mobile application/platform from scratch. Prizes will be awarded to the top hacks. There is no entrance fee and all meals are free.

FAQ

Who can attend?

Hack.UVA welcomes all current students. If you are an organization interested in sponsoring the event, please contact hackathon@virginia.edu.

When can I register?

Registration will be open Spring 2018! Stay tuned for details!

How big can my team be?

Between 3 and 7 feet tall. But seriously, you may work in a team with up to four people.

Bring your laptop, phone, and chargers!

How does judging work?

The judges will evaluate your hack based off creativity, polish, and technical vigor. Succeed in all three categories, and you're bound to win!
