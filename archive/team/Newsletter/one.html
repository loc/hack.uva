Getting involved with Hackers@UVA


Now that you have some background information, here’s a quick guide to help you decide if we'd be a good fit for you.

Getting accepted to Hackers@UVA automatically gives you a chance to play a large role in what we do.

We’re a small team, so each member has a large role to play. We have a competitive application process, but if you make it through, you will be a part of the team that hosts one of the biggest hackathons on the East Coast!

Do I need to be a CS major?

Nope - you just need to be passionate about technology, and ready to leverage your area of expertise to make our events even better.

What does the time commitment look like?

We have weekly hour-long meetings, which is the minimum amount of time you would have to devote through the year; as we get closer to the hackathon, the time commitment is more like 2-3 hours a week. Expect to be fully swamped the weekend of the hackathon.

Will it look good on my resume?

Yes.

Will I get to interact with companies and startups and build networks in the tech industry?

Yes.

OK - I’m convinced! How do I apply?

  1. Fill in this application form by the 26th of September
  2. Attend our interest meeting on the 19th of September, 6-8pm, in Thornton D221
  3. And you're all set!

If you clicked the link above and completed the application process - congratulations!! We’re excited to get to know you! If you think Hackers@UVA is not the best fit for you, that’s okay - you can still take advantage of our events and workshops by staying on our listserv.
